My City: AURANGABAD

GEOGRAPHY

Aurangabad town is located in north east Bihar. It is conected to Jharkhand Border, its nearest largest town is Gaya, here passes grand track roade which conects Delhi and Kolkata.

HISTORY

In ancient times, Aurangabad was located in the mahajanapada kingdom of magadh the ancient ruler was Bimbisara,Ajatashatru,Chandragupta maurya and Ashoka.

During the rule of sher shah suri Aurangabad became atrategically important as part of rohtash sirkar. After the death of Sher Shah Suri, Aurangabad fell under the rule of Akbar.
